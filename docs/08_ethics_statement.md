# Ethics Statement

This research does not perform medical diagnosis, mental-health assessment, employment evaluation, or academic evaluation.

## Scenario-Based Design

Study 1A does not induce actual presenteeism. Participants are asked to imagine a scenario in which they are not fully well but continue participating in work, school, or team activity.

## Manager-Visible Condition

The manager-visible condition is a scenario manipulation only. The app does not share participant responses with managers, teachers, employers, or any third party.

## Data Handling

The app stores responses in the browser's local storage and allows CSV/JSON export. It does not upload responses to a server.

If an online survey platform is used later, the paper must distinguish the experimental collection platform's data flow from the scenario system being evaluated.

## Free-Text Instruction

Participants must be instructed not to write:

- personally identifying information,
- names of other people or organizations,
- concrete disease names,
- diagnosis names.

## Debriefing

Debriefing should explain that all third-party visibility was hypothetical and used only to evaluate perceived monitoring, label imposition, and self-observation support.
